The Compassionate Heart of Mindfulness Masterclass

Led by Vidyamala Burch the founder of Breathworks and internationally renowned mindfulness expert and Ginny Wall the head of mindfulness training. This one-day masterclass provides you with all the tools you will need incorporate compassion within your mindfulness practice. Self-compassion involves treating yourself with the same kindness, concern and support you’d show to a good friend. Self-compassion can be a difficult to adopt as the world becomes more goal driven and individualistic. Studies have shown that those who utilise both mindfulness and self-compassion have developed greater emotional resilience and wellbeing. In addition to emotional benefits self-compassion has an indirect benefit to physical health seen in reduced stress.
